My experience

I consider that during the last few years and through projects I have been involved in developing, I have acquired knowledge that I never thought I would have. My first experience in the world of videogames and programming was Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles , an arcade game created with the SDL library and Visual Studio software. From that moment on, I improved my programming skills as I worked on more projects during my studies.

The project I was most impressed to have completed was a 3D game engine I developed along one of my colleagues, called Mist Engine . It was a really tough challenge to develop because it had a new level of difficulty in terms of programming, but it was really worth the hours invested for the result we got.

In all my previous jobs, I had never fully dealt with any area other than programming. So I feel very fortunate to be able to experiment in other areas, such as the design team of Guardians of The Galaxy: The Bet . This is the most important game for me so far. Although the development phase of the game was very challenging because it was my first experience leading the design team, the result is more than worthy, I'm very proud of the final result. Working as a team has been fundamental to achieve it, and each member has contributed their talent and dedication to make it happen.
